About Me

I am a passionate data analytics professional with over 15 years of experience transforming complex data into actionable insights. Rising quickly through leadership positions in government as a Financial Data Analyst and Cost Estimator, I excel at making data accessible and meaningful for decision-makers at all levels.

I'm particularly enthusiastic about leveraging open-source technologies to build cost-effective, scalable solutions that can be rapidly deployed. My technical expertise spans Python, R, SQL, machine learning, and data visualization, complemented by my work developing innovative tools that harness large language models and AI as strategic advantages for growing organizations.

I specialize in right-sizing data solutions for organizations, ensuring they maximize value without excessive infrastructure investments. My mission is to empower organizations with sustainable data capabilities that advance their goals and amplify their impact.
